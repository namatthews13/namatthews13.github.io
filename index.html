News and Updates

We are a research lab at Texas A&M University that is dedicated to developing electronic design automation (EDA) solutions to secure electronics chips from malicious attackers located anywhere in the semiconductor supply chain.
We follow a red-team blue-team approach that enables us to discover vulnerabilities in hardware as well as develop solutions to defend against known/anticipated attacks.
